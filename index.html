Mr Milne

Director

Welcome to DESMUN 2023 - the perfect platform for students to interpret, analyse and evaluate the world's problems in an environment that prepares them for solving the concerns of tomorrow. DESMUN is a great opportunity for students to interpret real-life issues that are challenging, provocative and at times, distressing and disturbing. However, they are embraced with a sense of ease and maturity. Under the guidance of this year's Big Four (Anushka, Delina, Purva and Tamara), whose perseverance and passion has enabled DESMUN'23 to grown into nine committees allowing more issues to be discussed, evaluated and analysed.. Alongside the Executive Team, without their desire and proficiency, DESMUN'23 would not be possible. Enjoy the conference.

Anushka

"Love yourself. Speak yourself. Be yourself."


Hello everyone, I am Anushka Kothekar, the Secretary General for Dukhan English School Model United Nations 2023.

When planning this year's conference, two words were definitely at the forefront of everything and they were “Renaissance” and “Phoenix”. The global pandemic and a worldwide lockdown has tainted our recent years. However, it is in times of uncertainty and confusion, that the phoenix becomes a symbol of strength, transformation, and renewal. Only through rising from the ashes of who we were, can we become who we are to be.

We have put together a transformative conference and I hope you will join us to see our hard work come to fruition.

Tamara

Deputy Secretary General

"Most great people have attained their greatest success one step beyond their greatest failure"


Hi everyone!

My name is Tamara, and I will be your Deputy Secretary General for DESMUN'23.

It is truly an honour for me to be a part of the Big4 and have the opportunity to organise this exceptional event alongside the rest of the executive team.

My favourite part of this journey so far has been working with our talented press team on developing the logo. After a very difficult time when we didn’t know when events like MUN would run again, we’re finally here, and so our logo celebrates the rebirth of DESMUN. We agreed that nothing would suit our theme better than the Phoenix; it rises from the ashes.

I am delighted to see our hard work pay off and to meet all of you on our new campus.

See you in March!
